“For unto you is born this day in the city of David a Savior, who is Christ the Lord.”

In this busy time of the year, let's not forget to remember the real reason for the season. John 3:16-17 says, “For God so loved the world, that he gave his only Son, that whoever believes in him should not perish but have eternal life. For God did not send his Son into the world to condemn the world, but in order that the world might be saved through him.”
