Life

The older I get the more I realize that the best analogy for life really is a roller coaster. I think the grandma in the movie Parenthood said it best when the main character, Gil (played by Steve Martin), was complaining about how complicated his life was. Grandma said:

“You know, when I was nineteen, Grandpa took me on a roller coaster. Up, down, up, down. Oh, what a ride! I always wanted to go again. You know, it was just so interesting to me that a ride could make me so frightened, so scared, so sick, so excited, and so thrilled all together! Some didn’t like it. They went on the merry-go-round. That just goes around. Nothing. I like the roller coaster. You get more out of it.”
