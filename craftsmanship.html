Craftsmanship

Wings: Making the Field Notes 2010 Fall Edition from Coudal Partners on Vimeo.

The older I get, the more I value American craftsmanship. At the risk of sounding like I am on a political soapbox, in a day and age where everything seems to be made in China, it is refreshing to see that Field Notes notebooks are well crafted and American made. This video is also very interesting to me because I love the behind the scenes look into how these notebooks are made.
