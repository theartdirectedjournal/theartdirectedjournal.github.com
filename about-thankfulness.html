What is this all about?

For those times when we feel like absolutely nothing is going right, a little perspective goes along way. But how do we gain that perspective? I have found that it helps to make a list of everything that I am grateful for. This way, when I am feeling like everything around me is falling apart, I can easily recall all the good things in my life.

I thought I would share my list with you. Each day, I will write down at least one thing that I am grateful for. Sometimes there will be a lot to write. Other times it may be a sentence or two. Sometimes it will be deep, like being thankful for a loving God, and other times, it will be very superficial.

Hopefully, this could be beneficial to someone in some way...
