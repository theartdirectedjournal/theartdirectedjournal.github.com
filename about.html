About The Journal

The premise of this journal is that every post is individually styled, like an article in a magazine. I was inspired to create my own after stumbling upon the article entitled "The Death of the Boring Blog Post?" on Smashing Magazine. I also drew inspiration from the pioneering art directed blogs of Paddy Donnelly, Dustin Curtis, Gregory Wood, and Jason Santamaria. This journal is also basically just an excuse to practice web design, play around with various CSS3 techniques, and (hopefully) improve my writing skills.

As for me, I have been blessed with a beautiful wife, two awesome boys, and a beautiful baby girl. I consider myself to be somewhat of a geek in the sense that I am into pretty much anything tech related.

Hope you enjoy. To get started, you can browse the Table of Contents.
