About The Journal

Hi, I'm Steve. The premise of this journal is that every post is individually styled, like an article in a magazine. I was inspired to create my own after stumbling upon the article entitled "The Death of the Boring Blog Post?" on Smashing Magazine. I also drew inspiration from the pioneering art directed blogs of Trent Walton, Gregory Wood, and Travis Neilson. Although art directed blogs (also sometimes called blogazines) are not as trendy as they once were, I find this blogging format to be quite enjoyable because it scratches my itch to design. As such, this journal is basically an excuse to practice web design, play around with various HTML and CSS (and maybe a little JavaScript) techniques, and (hopefully) improve my writing skills.

On a personal note, I have been blessed with a beautiful wife, two awesome boys, and a beautiful baby girl. I consider myself to be somewhat of a geek in the sense that I am into pretty much anything tech related. I love cars (anything Japanese, European and fast), and I like to play sports and video games with the boys.

I hope you enjoy the journal. To get started, you should browse the Table of Contents.
